The Horrors of War

One of out of every five displaced persons in the world is Syrian. Additionally, one out of two Syrians has fled home since the war began - or been killed. A staggering 4.1 million Syrians are registered refugees and need shelter from conflict, fatigue, and natural disasters.

Hosting for Hope

Asylum seekers are frantically searching for shelter and safety. Future Without Borders bridges the gap between hosts and refugees by providing an iOS interface for hosts and a text-message based service for refugees to accommodate any housing situation. Our product is 100% free to use and is due to the generosity of our host family partners

Whole Families Displaced

This crisis is especially tough for Syrian families. Family members have been killed in the conflict and fleeing the country can result in separation between parents, children, siblings, and relatives. Adapting to conditions in foreign countries is a daunting challenge, and refugees will generally have trouble finding employment and maintaining physical and mental health.
