What we do

Future without borders is a movement intended to merge people all across the globe regardless of race, creed, or beliefs. We hope you can help us trascend borders and provide assistance to those in need.

We are currently developing an iOS application intended for European citizens; they will be able to offer up their homes to refugees seeking asylum. We have additionally setup a text based server for refugees so they can contact us and get registered in our database.

We hope to ease the struggle the Syrian people are going through, and bridge the gap between peoples across the globe.
