What Do We Use Donations For?

To keep our website running

We need funds to maintain ownership of our domain, and host our website.

To maintain our servers

We require assistance to maintain our database and our web servers.

To fund our hosts

Sometimes our hosts require some additional funding to host refugees.

To maintain our iOS application

We require funds to keep our application on the app store.

Or Donate to these Organizations

  • Doctors Without Borders
  • International Rescue Committee
  • Save the Children
  • UN High Commissioner for Refugees

Doctors Without Borders

Providing refugees with everything from psychological care to lifesaving nutrition including setting up hospitals in refugee camps, helping women give birth safely, and providing access to safe drinking water.

International Rescue Committee

Providing humanitarian relief for refugees as they reach the shores of Greece and in Afganistan, Syria, and surrounding countries, as well as resettling thousands of refugees every year to the United States.

Save the Children

Working to keep refugee children safe and providing life-sustaining food, shelter kits, and supplies to keep families healthy.

UN High Commissioner for Refugees

Providing Syrian refugees with lifesaving assistance such as protection, shelter, food, water, and medical care and setting up reception centers where refugees can be registered.
